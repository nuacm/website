Members

Anyone can become a nuACM member! To become an official member of nuACM, we ask for a yearly membership due of $10.

Membership entitles you to a nuACM T-Shirt, (please request upon paying your dues), and access to our Library! There may also be some contests only for paying members. You'll also be listed on our website.

Want to join or renew your membership?

You can come to any ACM event and give your dues to the Treasurer for the school year, and they will take your information and give you a receipt. You will soon see yourself on this site and have access to all of the wonderful resources we have available for you!

Think you're already a member?

Your membership expires each school year. Each year you must pay your dues to be a member. Membership dues are $10.
